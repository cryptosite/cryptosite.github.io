Hello everyone,

I’m J.F. Hotte, CEO and founder of Cryptomen.com.

We are really proud to show you what we’ve been working on. Believe it or not, this fun adventure started in a community on Facebook, our Cryptomen FB group, in May 2014. It all started with myself and a few others making recommendations and analysis of coins. As our following grew, we knew we needed something better… A place where we could do more for our community, and leverage the ideas of the smart people we had acquired.

In june 2014, I decided to partner with Marie-Lynn Richard (CTO/DEV), I knew she had great experience in the crypto currencies world, coding and social media. We bought a domain and began working tirelessly to bring you the beautiful website you see today.

Coming from a business background, I knew I needed to team up with other great people for this project. I met Wesley D. Pryor, our amazing controler and Stéphane Beaudin who is working on our very own trading bot. We also have other advisors on our trading board to manage our portfolio and ensure the decisions we make are in the best interest of our business and community.

The original idea was to offer our following real insights and information about Bitcoin and Altcoins. We knew many traders were manipulated by whales and pump and dumps. We were doing very good trades and many members requested that we manage their coin portfolio’s for them, so we decided to offer our portfolio management services.

Cryptomen is one of the best places for alt-coin traders. We provide coin analysis, tips, fundamental analysis, TA’s, and recommendations from our members.

We started as an online community on social medias, and we are now offering: Portofolio Management, Trading tools, an informative blog about Bitcoin and trading, and CryptoTrade, with more innovative ideas on the way!

I want to personally thank you all for your support and encouragement to make Cryptomen the best alt-coin trading site on the web.

Stay tuned, as this is just the beginning!

Your friend,

J.F. Hotte
