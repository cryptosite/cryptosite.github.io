Conservative Vs. Aggressive Investors

What kind of investor are you?

At Cryptomen, we understand that every investor has a different risk appetite. This is why you will have the choice between a conservative portfolio or an aggressive portfolio. Trading decisions will be made consequently.  If you are a conservative trader, we will focus the majority of your investment portfolio off of less volatile currencies, Bitcoin rebalancing, and a smaller portion of your portfolio into riskier currencies.

Conversely, if you are an aggressive investor, we will focus your portfolio on small caps, Initial Public Offerings and more volatile currencies that we believe have the potential for abnormal returns. Not every investor will be considered wholly conservative or aggressive, that is why our investment advisers work closely with each client to ensure the best possible portfolio mix can be established.
