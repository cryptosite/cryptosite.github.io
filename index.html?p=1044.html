CRYPTOMEN ROADMAP

CryptoTrade

CryptoTrade is a full-featured social trading platform for our members. This system will allow users to keep a trading profile,  share their status, vote for coins, vote for bullish/bearish coins, connect their portfolios through an exchange’s API, recommend coins with opening and closing positions, send messages to other users and chat with other users. Users will be able to follow or copy trades made by other users who are showing great returns on our leaderboards. We wish to attract star users who will be able to offer paid professional consultation services to other members through our crypto-currency enabled platform. We want to be the e-Toro OpenBook of crypto-currency.

CryptoChat

Cryptomen hosts chats with interesting people developing the crypto-currrency technologies of tomorrow. Any group or person working on an interesting project can reserve a chat night and we will package and promote this as an event to our community.

We want to extend this service in the future by offering a platform so our members can access experts one-on-one. Whatever the topic, we want to attract experts who can be available to answer questions about crypto-currency usage, mining, trading, coding and security. We want to be the LivePerson of crypto-currency.

CryptoTrust

We are developing a leaderboard of coins with the top 100 coins (out of the 500 we track every 5 minutes). Our section can be ordered in many ways but will be heavy on our own exclusive metric, the Bear/Bull metric. This new metric is currently available in beta on our Weekly Recommendation Trading Board. The Bear/Bull metric will be deployed globally via a simple widget anyone can put on their website. We are working hard to protect this system from tampering so that it offers the crypto-currency community a valuable metric real-time sentiment analysis.
