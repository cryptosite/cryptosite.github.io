How Bitcoin Will Change the World

by Chris Naff | Nov 11, 2014 | Weekly Report |

With the dawn of the Internet, the ability to exchange funds has expanded rapidly. In a post-barter system world, payment systems have changed drastically. From the advent of credit and debit cards, to online payments and mobile banking, the manner in which we can process transactions looks very different from just 15 short years ago.

In a post-barter system world, however, changes in monetary systems have not been a common occurrence. Between gold, paper backed by gold, and fiat currencies, innovation in money has been sluggish at best. Major disruptions generally occur decades apart from one another. And one could argue that not every innovation in money has been to society’s benefit.

Enter stage right: Bitcoin.

Crypto-currencies are what the founders of PayPal originally set out to accomplish. PayPal wanted to give the world its first widely accessible digital currency. What we got instead was an innovative payment system. Perhaps dreaming of an alternative currency was a bit premature for 1999. But as innovative as PayPal was, money and payment systems were still separate.

That has changed. Crypto-currencies are paving the way for payments systems and money to become one system together, a more complete ecosystem. Previously, you have money and systems in place to exchange that money. Now, money has become a payment system.

In order for the idea of Bitcoin and other crypto-currencies to work at a large scale, the public has to trust that it will work in the long term. That, however, is secondary to another problem: many people (“most” is probably accurate too) still have no idea what Bitcoin is. That’s a problem, but not one that can’t be overcome with time.

When someone first learns about Bitcoin and other crypto-currencies, they have a difficult time wrapping their head around the concept. The success of Bitcoin and alternatives such as Litecoin, Dogecoin, or Darkcoin will depend on the ability for evangelists to find ways for the layman to understand the fundamentals. Once that happens, Bitcoins and other altcoins won’t be perceived as something only for the deep web and buying drugs from Colombia.

To accomplish that, the crypto-currency community needs to do a better job of explaining the benefit these systems can have on society. There has been a lot of explanation of how it works – the blockchain, wallets, mining, etc. But that’s very unexciting to the common folk. We’re trying to answer the wrong question. Instead, proponents of Bitcoin need to frame their argument by answering this question: what will it mean for the world if it does work? 

That is how people will understand these concepts. That is how Bitcoin will change the world.

Cryptomen Weekly Report – August 27, 2014

by J.F. Hotte | Aug 29, 2014 | Weekly Report |

Hello Traders,

Very interesting coins have emerged in the market lately. Not by the technicalities and specs, but mostly by the integration of a business backed by that crypto-currency. I love the idea to create new economies behind cryptoz, it’s not only about creating a coin for fun and launching it on Coinmarketcap anymore. There is more to crypto-currencies, I love those businessmen, those start-ups who wants to create, not only a coin, but a whole business model behind that coin. A lot of potential in that new way to promote crypto-currencies to the mainstream.

I’m talking about coins like StorjcoinX SJCX, I love that idea of using a crypto coin in a already proven business model (cloud storage). It is here to stay, there are so many utilities to it, I can’t sleep! Seriously, the concept is awesome, StorjX (the coin) is the monetary system chosen to reward (remunerate) people who share Hard Drive space for their Cloud Network Storj “Storage”.

We’ll see more and more of that just like.. NXTTY! Those guys are creating a messenger app similar to whatsapp, with other features like Voice and live news notifications. The idea of this to raise money (NXTTY) to develop a business like an iOS or android app.. is great! If they sell the app or create an ad system around the app with NXTTY coins, it might sky rocket. More details will be needed: if your buying the coins, will you own shares of the actual company if it goes public, or stay private. If anyone knows the answer, don’t hesitate to share your impression in the comments below.

Crypti, although there was a rough start, through no fault of their own (nxt got hijacked for 1.5 mil on Bter on XCR’s launch day), the developers have powered on and plowed through the obstacles they have faced. They now have a working wallet/forge available which is stable. A Windows one+click wallet release is imminent, blockchain explorers and more are also on the horizon. Keep any eye on this one as you can still find XCR under 20 sat’s right now on Cryptsy, Polo and Bter. They are still working hard on the development of their apps, a lot of uptrend potential, they released their roadmap.

Hope this is helping you making better trading decisions.

About Crypti

Crypti is a new crypto-currency designed from the ground up to solve the biggest problem with other currencies – lack of purchase motivation. Crypti is being built from scratch. It will not rely on any other crypto-currency code.

Crypti uses a combination of proof-of-stake algorithms making it first of a kind.

Crypti is being developed in lightweight Node.JS, and can run on every device out there, including embedded nodes.

Crypti’s Guaranteed Features

  • Custom block-chains, allowing anyone to launch their own chain with custom rules
  • Combination of 3 PoS algorithms:
  • Proof-of-Purchase (purchases done since the last won forged block)
  • Proof-of-Time (node running up-time)
  • Proof-of-Identity (merchant address real-life validation)
  • Ed25519 DSA for increased security.
  • 1 minute block time, pending on the network activity and node uptimes.
  • PoS retargeting on every new block.
  • Password protected wallets that allow recovery from seeds.
  • RESTful API for easy and fast interaction with Crypti nodes.
  • Development team is working on a merchant strategy and wants to innovate just like Stellar and Veribit. You will be able to purchase on e-commerce with Crypti converting them into bitcoin. They have a very nice business plan to enter this market.

There are 2 councils that will make the decisions for the BTC pre-sale investments. Shareholders, devs, and Cryptoz community elected members. They have democratic vision, taking decisions and sharing ideas with everyone.
